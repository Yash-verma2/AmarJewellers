Our Legacy

Preserving Artistry Since 1995

Amar Jewellers was founded with a singular vision: to create jewellery that is as enduring as the stories it celebrates. For over nearly three decades, we have been Siwan's most trusted name in fine jewellery.

Our journey is one of passion, precision, and an unwavering commitment to purity. Every piece that leaves our atelier is a symphony of traditional craftsmanship and contemporary elegance.

01

Uncompromising Purity

We only use BIS Hallmarked gold and IGI/GIA certified diamonds. Transparency isn't just a promise; it's our heritage.

02

Master Artistry

Our artisans spend hundreds of hours on a single piece, ensuring that every curve and setting is a work of perfection.

03

Bespoke Experience

From private consultations to custom designs, we ensure your journey with us is as precious as the jewellery you choose.
